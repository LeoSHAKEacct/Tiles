How a Job Runs

Four steps. No surprises.

We walk every client through the same sequence, so you always know what's next and what it costs.

Step 1

Consultation

We measure your space, talk through material options, and give you a firm quote — not an estimate that grows.

Step 2

Material & Layout

Pick your tile or stone, plan the pattern, and lock the schedule before any demo starts.

Step 3

Installation

Substrate prep, waterproofing where needed, precise setting, and clean grout lines from day one.

Step 4

Final Walkthrough

We inspect every line with you on site before we call the job done — not before.
